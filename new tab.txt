This targets the Japanese 4th-grade standard for mastering the order of operations (parentheses, multiplication/division, addition/subtraction) and the properties of operations (commutative, associative, and distributive properties) to calculate efficiently.

3. Topic Selection & Pre-Exercise Explanations (計算のきまり Tab)

    Topic 1: 計算のじゅんじょ / Order of Operations

        Goal / めあて: Solve math problems in the correct boss-battle order! / 正しいじゅんじょで計算して、答えを導き出そう！

        How it Works / やりかた: 1. ( ) inside parentheses first. / ( ) の中を一番はじめに計算する。
        2. × and ÷ next. / かけ算(×)とわり算(÷)を次に計算する。
        3. + and - last. / たし算(+)とひき算(-)は最後。

        Real-Life / 生活の中でのつかいみち: Like in an RPG: You equip armor ( ) first, attack enemies × ÷ next, and collect gold + - at the end! / RPGのゲームと同じ！最初に装備( )をつけて、敵を攻撃(×÷)して、最後にコイン(+)を拾うんだ！

        Exercise Generation Logic: Generate equations requiring 2 or 3 steps.

            Type A: A + B × C (e.g., 20 + 5 × 4)

            Type B: (A - B) ÷ C (e.g., (30 - 10) ÷ 5)

        Prompt format: "Calculate the following. / 次の計算をしましょう。" (Expected input: The final integer answer).

    Topic 2: くふうして計算しよう / Calculate Smartly (Commutative & Associative)

        Goal / めあて: Swap or group numbers to make calculations incredibly fast. / 数を入れ替えたりまとめたりして、計算をパッと終わらせよう。

        How it Works / やりかた: Find pairs that make exactly 10, 100, or 1000. Example: 25 × 4 = 100. / ぴったり10、100、1000になる数のペアを見つけるのがコツ！

        Real-Life / 生活の中でのつかいみち: Organizing your inventory so similar items stack perfectly up to 99! / アイテムボックスの中で、同じ種類のアイテムをきれいにまとめるのと同じだよ！

        Exercise Generation Logic: Generate 3-part addition or multiplication equations where two non-adjacent numbers form a clean multiple of 10 or 100.

            Addition: A + B + C where A + C = 100 (e.g., 18 + 55 + 82).

            Multiplication: A × B × C where A × C = 100 or 1000 (e.g., 25 × 13 × 4 or 125 × 7 × 8).

        Prompt format: "Calculate smartly. / くふうして計算しましょう。" (Expected input: The final integer answer).

    Topic 3: 分配法則 (まとめる・配る) / Distributive Property

        Goal / めあて: Break down hard numbers into easy numbers before multiplying. / むずかしい数を、計算しやすい簡単な数に分けてからかけ算しよう。

        How it Works / やりかた: 98 × 5 is the same as (100 - 2) × 5, which is 100 × 5 - 2 × 5. / 98 × 5 は (100 - 2) × 5 と同じ。100 × 5 - 2 × 5 に分けて計算できるよ。

        Real-Life / 生活の中でのつかいみち: Buying a 98-coin sword for 5 party members? Pretend it's 100 coins each, then subtract the 2-coin difference! / 98コインの剣を5人分買うとき、「100コインを5人分」と考えてから、おつりを計算すると早いよ！

        Exercise Generation Logic: Generate multiplication problems where one multiplier is close to 100 (e.g., 97, 98, 99, 101, 102) and the other is a single digit (2-9).

        Prompt format: Provide step-by-step inputs to guide the logic.

            Equation: 98 × 4

            Step 1: ( [input1] - 2 ) × 4 (Expected input: 100)

            Step 2: [input2] - 8 (Expected input: 400)

            Final Answer: [input3] (Expected input: 392)
